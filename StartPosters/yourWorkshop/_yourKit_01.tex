\subsection{Introduction}\label{introduction}

Done as a team. Please appoint one person to take notes (or refer to
prior notes) on what the team likes in a poster. If your team is clever,
you can include the notes in the poster. Otherwise notes are just for
your records, no need to turn them in.

Please choose at least one of the templates (they can be found in the
\_docs folder) and copy and modify it.

The available templates can be found in the following folders within the
\_docs folder, we recommend downloading the entire folder to insure
correct formatting.:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  betterPoster (has been tested on Overleaf)
\item
  betterPoster\_RMarkdown (has been tested in RStudio)
\item
  DoStemplate (has been tested on Overleaf)
\item
  Jacobs\_RMarkdown (has been tested in RStudio)
\item
  quarto\_typst (has been tested in RStudio \& VS Code but need to add a
  quarto extension to make work see
  https://github.com/quarto-ext/typst-templates/tree/main/poster)
\end{itemize}

You may need to run the following two lines at the console prompt in R
in order to get betterPoster\_RMarkdown to render:

\begin{verbatim}
install.packages("devtools")
devtools::install_github("gerkelab/betterposter")
\end{verbatim}

\subsection{Your Work}\label{your-work}

Please upload your team's attempt at getting a poster started
\textbf{today} by rendering a modified poster template and the poster
output (PDF) should have your team photo on it and your names, that's
all. I just need to know that a poster will render on at least one of
your computers.

The remainder are some suggested modifications:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Please attempt as many of the following modifications as you are able

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    Modified Title
  \item
    Modify a section title
  \item
    Modify text or add a paragraph of text to the body of the poster
  \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
